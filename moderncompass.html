Modern Compass

Web/Mobile Application - July 2017

Role : Web/Mobile Designer & Product Design

A new upcoming app that will make outdoor exploring safer.

What is Modern Compass?

Modern Compass is a cross-platform outdoor activity companion app for all levels. The mobile app features assigning an emergency contact with the abilities to track the users GPS location and footprints.

Workflow

The project is being developed by a four man team. This project utilizes crowd sourcing to support the various amount of outdoor activities for users.

Through agile methodologies, my role is to make sure we are developing an application that users want. In each phase of production, we perform usability testing for each feature and conclude whether our not the feature is essential to our goals in Modern Compass.

Personas

I conduct interviews with our target audience to build personas which identify design needs of potential users.

Mobile Application UI

IOS / Android

I drafted numerous wireframes with paper and pencil before creating mockups in Adobe Experience Design CC. Through design reviews, we achieved a simple, smooth, modern UI based on Material Design.

UI Design

Web Application

Conclusion

Modern Compass is still in development and plans to release the beta in December. The design of Modern Compass is always evolving with the current trends. This team is very passionate about this project and will always be moving towards the best designs to best mesh with our target audience and application.
