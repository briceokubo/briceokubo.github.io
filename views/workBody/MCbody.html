Modern Compass

Web/Mobile Application - July 2017

Role : Web/Mobile Designer & Product Design

A new upcoming app that will make outdoor exploring safer.

What is Modern Compass?

Modern Compass is a cross-platform outdoor activity companion app for all levels. The mobile app features assigning an emergency contact with the abilities to track the users gps location and footprints.

Workflow

The project is being develop by a small team. This project utilized crowd sourcing to introduce support for the enormous amount of outdoor activities for users.

Through agile methodologies, my role is to make sure we are developing an application that users want. In each phase of production, we usability test each features and conclude whether our not the feature is essential or does not follow our goals in Modern Compass.

Personas

I conducted with interview our target audience to build personas which identified design needs of potential users.

Mobile Application UI

IOS / Android

I drafted a numerous wireframe with paper and pencil before creating mockups in Adobe Experience Design CC. Through design reviews we ended up at a simple, smooth modern UI based on Material Design

UI Design

Desktop

The desktop app compliments the mobile app to present users with a forum community for users to connect and plan events.

Conclusion

Modern Compass is still in development and plans to release the beta in December. From a web designer standpoint, this project and team is very passionate and will always be moving towards the best designs to best mesh with our target audience and application.
