TecHub

E-Commerce Website - November 2016

Role : Web Designer

A website redesign to kickoff the launch of a new University of Hawaii Bookstore department

http://www.bookstore.hawaii.edu/manoa/techub

Project Overview

University of Hawaii Manoa Bookstore is now allowing students, faculty and staff to purchase Apple products with educational pricing through their E-commerce site. With the big news, the computer department rebrands itself to TecHub and a complete redesign of the department webpage.

Goals

With direction from store managers and personal experience using the computer department webpage, our team set out to accomplish three goals:

  • Useful navigation
  • Consistent design with other department webpages
  • Easily maintainable

Wireframe

Desktop

My focus was providing simple interface that will help guide users to the right section they are targeting. So, I did some initial research on the target users, on which products and questions are frequently asked via in person and on telephone.

Based on my research, we were able to validate the three main sections that users are frequently targeting and able to place them in a quick link on the homepage for direct access.

Desktop

Design Solution

Though not all computer products are available through the e-commerce store, we are required to display the other products through other methods. With the constant change of products that we bring in, the marketing department is not able to keep up to date. I created a solution that utilizes the old department CMS to host the PDFs pages that will display the non-e-commerce products linked from the new TecHub page.


Conclusion

The reimagned TecHub has received praise from students, faculty, and staff. The new feature to purchase an Apple computer from the bookstore website has been positively received by parents and fiscal officers because of the convenient, streamline process; an improvement from credit card transactions over the phone.
