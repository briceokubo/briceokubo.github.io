A Beauty Website

Web Application - August 2017

Role : Web Designer/ Product Designer

A web application that help teach and provide a new way you look up 10 step

Project Overview

Project A Beauty website, is a collaboration with a good friend, who graduated in ICS degree, who wants to create an application for his current hobby/interest of 10 steps beauty care. With a general idea of what he wants to accomplish from the application, but for me who does not know very much of the product itself, wont be able to effectively create a web design and great user experience without this knowledge. So to help both my collaborator and myself, we did a product thinking exercise in order for both us to truly understand our goals and vision.

Product Thinking

From very insightful article from the medium, link, i used the product thinking chart for us to answer.

Once these question was answered, it was a great opportunity for my collaborator and myself to truly realize what his intentions was and how they have changed from answering these question.

Personas

Once we discovered, who our targeted audience are, we create personas based on our self and friend who is also interested in the application. Which was perfect, because we targeted people of all three level of beginner, intermediate and advance in the knowledge of 10 steps.

Scenarios

With scenarios we try to bring out future predictions of users actions based on personas.

Conclusion

This project was a great experience for myself. From my experience in UX/UI design and IT support of the department, I was able to translate my knowledge and experience with customers to the UX design of the website to target the right audience.
