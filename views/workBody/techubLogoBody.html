TecHub Logo

Branding - Feburary 2017

Role : Graphic Designer

A new logo for a the redesign Computer department, TecHub

Hike Aloha

What is TecHub?

TecHub is a newly branded redesign of the old Computer Department in the University of Hawai’i Bookstore. The department specializes in technology retail, repair service, and quick IT support for students, faculty, and staff.

Experimental

Sketches & Mockups

The mock-ups consisted of playing with different style of logos in order to get a better idea of what the bookstore was looking for, including shapes, fonts, and colors.

New Direction

After experimentation with the University logo, the bookstore wanted to utilize the H logo for easy recognition. We gained permission to use and modify the H logo, avoiding copyright issues and overall achieving an identity for TecHub.

Conclusion

Approved by the copyright owner of the “H” logo, the TecHub department’s final decision regarding its logo design was made. This is now the new face of the TecHub department.
