Sketches & Mockups

The original sketches are playing with the broad idea of technology. The mockups was just playing with different style of logo, just to get a better idea of what type of logo they are looking for. Whether it was the shape, idea, font or colors.

New Direction

After all the experiments logo, the bookstore wanted to utilize the H logo for the university for easy recognition. But due to copyright of the H logo, the bookstore was forbidden from using the H logo. With permission we are able to modify the "H" to create our own "H" identity for TecHub.
