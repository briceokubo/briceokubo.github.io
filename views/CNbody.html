Carbon Neutrality Challenge

Case Study - December 2015

A web application set out to change the world by calcuating your carbon emission.

Mora Lab

Site Flow

With a team of three memebers, we gather all the features for the web application, and I created the wireframe/ sitemap.

Design a web application to keep track of users carbon emission to help save the world! Camilo Mora, University of Hawaii Manoa Professor, is spreading news of a new upcoming web application to help you keep track of the trees you plant to counter act the carbon emission you create.

Wireframe

Mock Up

UX Solution

While creating the wireframes and attempting to track my own carbon emission, I came across issues of trying input my own data in the carbon calculator. A lot of the fillable field, required information that would not be easily answered by a child or adult. A guide or help button would be needed to help user estimate the amount of food, miles, and etc information needed to calculate your emission.

Conclusion

At the time of the completion of the wireframes and mock up, Professor Mora was not ready on the server side of the his web application. As a gift, we were happy to give our design scheme as gift to him and his future plans for Carbon Neutrality Challenge.
