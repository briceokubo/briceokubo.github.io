TecHub

E-Commerce Website - November 2016

Role : Web Designer

A website redesign to kickoff the launch of a new University of Hawaii Bookstore department

http://www.bookstore.hawaii.edu/manoa/techub

Project Overview

University of Hawaii Manoa Bookstore is now allowing students, faculty and staff to purchase Apple products with educational pricing through their E-commerce site. With the big news, the computer department rebrands itself to TecHub and redesigned the department webpage.

Goals

Through personal experience using the computer department webpage and direction from the store managers, our team set out to accomplish three goals:

  • Simple navigation
  • Consistent design with other department webpages
  • Easily maintainable

Wireframe

Desktop

My focus was providing simple, user friendly interface that will guide users to their desired section. I did some initial research on the target audience, on which products and questions are frequently asked via in person and on telephone.

Based on my research, we were able to validate the three main sections that users are frequently targeting and able to place them in a quick link on the homepage for direct access.
